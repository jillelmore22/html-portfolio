Jill's Favorite Fall Movies

Here is a list of my favorite movies that are perfect for the autumn season!

Hocus Pocus

Starting off with a halloween movie, Hocus Pocus is a classic that is so entertaining and nostalgic.

Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire

All of the Harry Potter movies are great for the fall season, but I do like the Goblet of Fire for its interesting plot and new characters.

You've Got Mail

You've Got Mail is the perfect autumn rom com staring Tom Hank and Meg Ryan...what more could you ask for?
